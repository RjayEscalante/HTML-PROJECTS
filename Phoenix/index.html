The Order of the Phoenix is a group of fiery computer science students at Gordon College, united by our burning passion for technology. We are a diverse group with a range of skills and interests, but what truly sets us apart is our fierce determination to stay at the forefront of the field.

As members of the Order of the Phoenix, we are not afraid to rise from the ashes and take on new challenges. We participate in hackathons, work on cutting-edge research projects, and collaborate with industry partners to explore the latest trends and technologies in computer science.
